# Background Music for AdForge

You need to add 4 royalty-free music files to this directory:

## Required Files:
- `upbeat.mp3` - Upbeat & Energetic music
- `corporate.mp3` - Corporate & Professional music
- `calm.mp3` - Calm & Relaxing music
- `inspiring.mp3` - Inspiring & Motivational music
